Personal budgeting and financial reporting tool.

Budgr brings transaction data from all my financial accounts into one place using Plaid, giving me a clean, unified ledger. It helps me build budgets based on actual spending habits and sends me daily email updates on how I'm tracking against goals.

Terms of Service

Budgr is an app developed for my personal use. I'll update the terms of service if I decide to scale Budgr to other users.

Privacy Policy

I developed Budgr to reduce third party access to my financial data. I'll keep my data private and safe. I don't intend to sell or share my data.
